Ricotta Pancakes

By Candice
Cook Time Calories Serves
20 1000 6-12 People

Recipe Key

Vegetarian
Can use dairy alternative.

Ingredients

1 egg
45 mL/g milk (dairy or non-dairy)
10 g of sugar
40 g of self-raising flour

  1. Separate egg white from egg yolk, place egg white in a smaller separate bowl

  2. Add and mix milk, sugar and self-raising flour with egg yolk

  3. Whisk egg white until stiff peaks form

  4. Fold egg white in with the rest of the ingredients

  5. Heat non-stick pan with low-medium heat

  6. Add batter onto the pan, and flip once the edges harden and bubbles form on the top of the batter

  7. Enjoy with toppings of your liking!
